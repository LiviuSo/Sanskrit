### **Sanskrit Verse (IAST)**

**śrī-bhagavān uvāca**

**imaṁ vivasvate yogaṁ proktavān aham avyayam |**

**vivasvān manave prāha manur ikṣvākave’bravīt || 4.1 ||**

---

| **Sanskrit Word**   | **Grammar & Meaning**                                                              |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **śrī-bhagavān** | *compound noun, nominative singular (masculine)*– "the Blessed Lord" (Krishna)        |
| **uvāca**          | *verb, perfect, 3rd person singular*– "said, spoke"                                   |
| **imaṁ**           | *pronoun, accusative singular (masculine)*– "this"                                    |
| **vivasvate**       | *noun, dative singular (masculine)*– "to Vivasvān (the sun god)"                     |
| **yogam**           | *noun, accusative singular (masculine)*– "yoga, the spiritual discipline"             |
| **proktavān**      | *past active participle, nominative singular (masculine)*– "declared, taught"         |
| **aham**            | *pronoun, nominative singular*– "I"                                                   |
| **avyayam**         | *adjective, accusative singular (masculine/neuter)*– "imperishable, eternal"          |
| **vivasvān**       | *noun, nominative singular (masculine)*– "Vivasvān (the sun god)"                    |
| **manave**          | *noun, dative singular (masculine)*– "to Manu (the progenitor of mankind)"            |
| **prāha**          | *verb, perfect, 3rd person singular*– "told, spoke"                                   |

| **ikṣvākave**     | *noun, dative singular (masculine)*– "to Ikṣvāku (first king of the solar dynasty)" |
| **abravīt**        | *verb, perfect, 3rd person singular*– "spoke, told"                                   |

---

### **Reconstructed Word Order (Prose Form in Sanskrit)**

**śrī-bhagavān uvāca –**

**aham imaṁ avyayam yogam vivasvate proktavān,**

**vivasvān manave prāha,**

**manuḥ ikṣvākave abravīt.**

---

### **Structured Translation (Following the Sanskrit Syntax Closely)**

* **"The Blessed Lord said" (śrī-bhagavān uvāca),**
* **"I" (aham) "taught" (proktavān) "this" (imaṁ) "eternal yoga" (avyayam yogam) "to Vivasvān (the sun god)" (vivasvate).**
* **"Vivasvān" (vivasvān) "told" (prāha) "it to Manu" (manave),**
* **"and Manu" (manuḥ) "told" (abravīt) "it to Ikṣvāku" (ikṣvākave).**

---

### **Final Smooth Translation**

"The Blessed Lord said: I taught this eternal yoga to Vivasvān (the sun god); Vivasvān told it to Manu, and Manu told it to Ikṣvāku."
